ScanFest

This was a project I started back in March 2020, it build to head solve issues arise from chilldren that are picked from school by drivers and children care givers.

Once a driver or a child care give drop a child in school he/she will be made to scan for a barcode which automatically send sms to the parent with the timestamp the child was drop and pick from school.

This project is still in progress but it has reached 70% you can check at https://github.com/dakony/scanfest.

Technologies:
