Carlink

This was a project I started back in August 2019, it build to rise awearness by the users of the app whenever the notice some security threats on parked vehicles.

Once a user notice some suspicious activity around a parked vehicle he can post on the platform and notification will be send to the users of the app and he can make call to nearest police.

This project is has been completed and submitted to university of jos as undergraduate project you can find it here check at https://github.com/dakony/ipark.
